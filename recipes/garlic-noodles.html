San Francisco Garlic Noodles

This strange fusion of italian and asian cuisine is such a treat! This dish has been a staple California street food for years and once you try it you will easily see why!

Perfectly cooked spaghetti noodles are tossed with a medley of asian sauces, a truckload of crushed garlic, and finished with a hearty sprinkle of parmesan cheese. Garnish with chopped green onions and you have yourself a super simple weeknight dish that will satisfy anybody's garlic craving.

    Ingredients

  • 4 TBSP unsalted butter
  • 20 cloves crushed or grated garlic
  • 4 tsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1lb dry spaghetti
  • 1oz grated parmesan
  • sliced green onion for garnish

    Steps

  1. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Add oyster sauce, soy sauce, and fish sauce, stir to combine. Remove from heat
  2. Bring water to boil over high heat. add pasta and cook, stirring occasionally until just shy of al dente
  3. Transfer cooked pasta to garlic sauce in skillet. increase heat to high, add cheese, stir and toss pasta vigorously until sauce is creamy. Stir in scallions and serve
