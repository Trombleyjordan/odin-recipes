Lasagna

This wonderful family favorite pasta is perfect for a crowd! Making the pasta from scratch can add an extra layer of fun for the whole family!

This lasagna has been in my family's rotation for years now it includes 3 cheeses a hearty meat sauce perfect for a fall time dinner!

Ingredients

    What you will need:
  • Fresh made or store bought lasagna noodles
  • Fresh made or store bought meat sauce
  • Mozzarella, parmesan, and pecorino cheeses, grated and mixed
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil for garnish

    Steps

  1. Boil your pasta noodles until package instructions for al dente
  2. layer meat sauce on bottom of baking dish
  3. add a layer of pasta noodles, a layer of cheese mixture, and more sauce
  4. alternate in this pattern until you've filled the baking dish
  5. finish the top layer with one more serving of meat sauce and the rest of your cheese mixture
  6. bake in 400 degree oven for 2 hours or heated through
  7. garnish with fresh chopped basil when out of the oven
  8. let cool, slice into squares, and enjoy!
