Cherry Tomato Pasta

This quick and easy pasta dish is perfect for weeknights!

Cherry tomatoes are the perfect way to make a quick red sauce with fresh tomatoes. The small size of cherry tomatoes ensure a burst of flavor even when out of season.

    Ingredients

  • 1lb dry pasta
  • kosher salt
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced thin
  • 6 TBSP evoo
  • 2 pints cherry tomatoes
  • 1oz fresh basil
  • black pepper
  • parmesan cheese

    Directions

  1. boil pasta of choice just shy of al dente
  2. heat garlic with 4 TBSP of olive oil in skillet over medium heat until garlic is softened. Add tomatoes and cook until tomatoes begin to burst, 10 minutes
  3. Continue to coo until sauce is rich and creamy. Stir in basil and season to taste with salt and pepper
  4. drain pasta reserving 1c of pasta water. Add pasta to sauce and increase to medium-high. Cook, stirring and tossing adding pasta water as necessary to adjust consistency. Remove from heat stir in 2 TBSP evoo and a generous shower of parmesan cheese
